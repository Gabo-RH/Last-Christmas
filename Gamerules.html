🎲 Game Rules 🎮

  • 🏁 1. All players begin with 0
  • 👥 2. Min player to play is 4 max player to play is 8
  • 👤 3. By every player you have to deliver 5 gift(20 If you are 4 40 if you are 8)
  • 🎁 4. Players work together to deliver gifts.
  • 🎲 5. Each player takes turns to roll a die and move around the cel
  • ⏰ 6. Each player has to work together and deliver x amount of gift to Santa
  • 🚫 7. If a player lands on a Grinch cel you have to take a grinch card
  • 🎁 8. If a player lands on a gift cel you have to take a gift card
  • ⏳ 9. you have 25 minutes to deliver all gifts to santa so be quick and work together!
  • 🚫 10. If time runs out, the Grinch wins!
  • 🎁 11. If all players deliver all the gifts. Everyone automatically wins! 🎉
  • 🎅🏻 12. Complete objectives to save Christmas! 🎁🎄
